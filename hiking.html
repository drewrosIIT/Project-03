Hiking

Description

Hiking is another one of my hobbies. I enjoy exploring and being in nature. When I hike, I enjoy taking in the scenery and the landscapes, and seeing new places.

Benefits

  1. See beautiful scenery and landscapes
  2. Exercise and Physical Activity
  3. Wildlife Sightings
  4. Sense of accomplishment
  5. Determination and perseverance are necessary

Favorite Hikes and Experiences

Waterfall Hike

Above I am at a waterfall hike. Waterfall hikes are interesting to me because you start to hear the water as you approach the fall, and then the end is an exciting big reveal!

Yellowstone National Park

  1. Old Faithful Geyser
  2. Mount Washburn
  3. Spotting A Wolf

Yellowstone is my favorite national park for a number of reasons. Yellowstone was the first national park that I went to. It was very special for me to be exploring in the Wyoming mountains. Also, it was fun to see Old Faithful in person. I also experienced many great hikes around the park, and enjoyed the time with my family. Lastly, I loved all of the wildlife. I saw herds of bison, mooses, and more. I even spotted a wolf once when driving back from a hike in the evening. It was very cool to see the wolf appear in the trees on the side of the road, and how it acted. Yellowstone will always be a very special place to me!

Glacier National Park

  1. Iceberg Lake
  2. Logan Pass
  3. Going to the Sun Road

I think that each national park is incredibly special and unique. When I hike in a different park, the experiece is different each time, but still amazing. For instance, when I was in Zion National Park, it was 100 degrees Farenheight every day. However, when I was in Glacier National Park, it was actively snowing at the top of a big peak in July! In each park, I can always experience something new!
